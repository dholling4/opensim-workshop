Prerequisites

This workshop is designed for graduate students, post-graduate students, and researchers with the following background:

  • Graduate, post-graduate students, or researchers
  • Proficient in 3D motion capture, biomechanical modelling, and inverse dynamics (e.g. joint moments)
  • Basic coding experience in MATLAB or Python
  • Bring your own laptop (Windows, Mac, or Linux)
  • OpenSim software installed (instructions will be provided upon registration)

What You Will Learn

Gain comprehensive skills in computational biomechanics and musculoskeletal modeling.

  • Fundamentals of musculoskeletal modeling workflow in OpenSim
  • Personalizing generic musculoskeletal model with motion capture inputs
  • Inverse kinematics
  • Static optimization and joint reaction force analysis
  • Result validation
